The Murder of Victor Black

Victim: Victor Black

Cause of Death: Poisoning (Arsenic)

Motive: Inheritance dispute

Evidence: A broken glass with traces of poison and a threatening letter found in Victor's office.

Suspects

1. Alice Black - Victor's estranged sister, known for her gambling debts.
2. Robert Gray - Victor's business partner, bitter over recent business decisions.
3. Linda Greene - Victor's ex-fiancée, upset about their broken engagement, desperate for money.
4. Thomas Black - Victor's son, fighting for control of the family business.
5. Sarah Collins - Housekeeper, had a secret affair with Victor, been cut off financially since their breakup.
6. George White - A distant cousin who believed he was entitled to part of Victor's estate.

Police Inspector

Inspector: James Ford, sharp and observant, determined to find the truth.
