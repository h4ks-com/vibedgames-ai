3) Conceptual brand and universe bible

3.1 Premise & logline

One-line premise and a concise logline anchor the universe and the playable ad experience.

Premise: Gravitas-led mission aboard a spacefaring toilet platform to demonstrate warp and containment tech in the most cinematic way.

3.2 Tone, audience, culture

Tone blends sci-fi gravitas with light humor; audience includes adults 18–45, global, with careful cultural portrayal of Chinese talent and tech culture.

3.3 Visual language bible

Color palette

Cobalt, neon cyan, and warm gold accents; contrast for legibility in space-lit environments.

Typography

Sans family for UI; monospaced for telemetry; occasional serif in titles to signal gravitas.

Materials & lighting

Brushed metals, glass, programmable LEDs; rim lighting to emphasize silhouettes.

Signage & UI language

Clear symbol language for warp paths, containment, and diagnostics.

3.4 Brand mythology

The Level 74 toilet is a centerpiece with ethical stances and environment-conscious tech that defines the world.

4) Narrative design and script development

4.1 Ad script (60–90 seconds) and cuts

1. Opening: Captain Li Wei Sol addresses the audience from the capsule, introducing Warp Flush as a leap in cosmic hygiene. 2. Cut to: Warp tunnels opening like a city-wide artery, UI telemetry highlights safety interlocks. 3. Climax: The Level 74 toilet demonstrates an elegant containment sequence that collapses waste into harmless glittering stardust. 4. CTA: Visit the full game demo to explore warp paths and unlock upgrades.

4.2 Playable trailer narrative

A teaser showing core mechanics: observe environment, activate warp, solve environmental puzzles, collect telemetry credits, unlock upgrades. Narrative threads weave through the ad and the game world.

4.3 Characters & backstories

Lead: fictional Captain Li Wei Sol, a gravitas-forward commander. Supporting engineers and AI companions have distinct silhouettes and voices. Antagonists are systemic: malfunctions, bureaucracy, and a rogue firmware parasite that tests ethics.

4.4 Localization style sheets

Pacing: tight, with quotes and slogans rendered as telemetry bursts. Tone preserved across languages with careful translation of techno-slang.
