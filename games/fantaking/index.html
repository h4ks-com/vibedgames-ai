🔷 Hexagonal Wang Tiles

Constraint Rules:
Each hex tile has 6 edges, each assigned one of 4 colors:
🟢 GND (ground)   🔵 AIR (open)   🔵💧 H2O (water)   🔴 WALL (solid)

Matching: Opposite edge pairs must share colors across neighbors:
• N ↔ S  • NE ↔ SW  • SE ↔ NW

The solver uses backtracking to guarantee all constraints are satisfied.
