Game Instructions

Controls

  • W / A / S / D: Move Forward / Left / Backward / Right
  • Mouse: Aim / Look around
  • Left Click: Shoot
  • ESC: Toggle this instructions screen

Objectives

Navigate the game environment, eliminate enemies by shooting them, and survive as long as possible to achieve a high score. Keep an eye on your health and ammo!

Tips

  • Use cover to avoid enemy fire.
  • Conserve ammo; pick up ammo packs when available.
  • Practice aiming with the mouse for better accuracy.
  • Try to keep moving to evade enemies.
