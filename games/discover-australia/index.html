History Timeline

~65,000 years ago
Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples arrive — the world's oldest continuous living culture.
1606
Dutch navigator Willem Janszoon makes first recorded European landing on Australian soil.
1770
Captain James Cook claims the east coast for Great Britain and names it "New South Wales."
1788
The First Fleet arrives at Sydney Cove, establishing the first British penal colony.
1813
Lawson, Blaxland & Wentworth cross the Blue Mountains — opening the inland for settlement. 🏔️
1851
Gold rush begins, sparking massive immigration and population boom.
1901
The six colonies federate to form the Commonwealth of Australia.
1915
ANZAC forces land at Gallipoli — a defining moment in Australian national identity.

Iconic Wildlife

🦘
Kangaroo
National symbol. Over 50 million roo's hop across the country.
🐨
Koala
Sleeps 18-22 hours a day. Eucalyptus leaves are their only diet.
🦆
Platypus
One of the few mammals that lay eggs. Has venomous ankle spurs!
🐊
Saltwater Croc
The world's largest living reptile — males can exceed 6 metres.
🦈
Great White Shark
Found along southern coasts. Up to 6m long and 2,000kg.
🦜
Cockatoo
Loud, intelligent, and long-lived — some species live 70+ years.
🕷️
Huntsman Spider
Harmless to humans but terrifying looking. Legspan up to 30cm!
🐢
Green Sea Turtle
Found on the Great Barrier Reef. Can live over 80 years.
