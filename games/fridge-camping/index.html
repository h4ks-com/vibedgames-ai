❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe? ▼
Look, we're not going to lie to you — our legal team strongly advised against answering this question. But statistically, you're more likely to be injured by a falling coconut than by our tents. Probably. We haven't done the math because math is hard and cold is easy.
What if my roommate needs a sandwich? ▼
All our tents include the patented SnackPass™ window — a zippered portal that allows sandwich retrieval without breaking your thermal seal. Your roommate gets their ham & cheese, you keep your chill. Everybody wins (except the electricity bill).
Will this increase my electricity bill? ▼
Yes. Significantly. Like, worryingly so. But think of it this way: you could spend $300/month on AC, or $400/month on fridge camping AND look cool doing it. It's not about saving money. It's about making a statement.
Do you ship internationally? ▼
We ship everywhere except Antarctica — they don't need us and honestly we respect that. Shipping takes 3-7 business days, or 2-3 weeks if customs asks what a "refrigerated tent" is (they always ask).
What's your return policy? ▼
30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return the tent for a full refund. Note: "the ice cream melted" is not a valid reason for return. That's on you, Gary.
Can I use this with a mini-fridge in my dorm room? ▼
Absolutely! In fact, our "I'm Fine" Desk Tent was specifically designed for exactly this scenario. Your RA will never know you're living inside a Crigidaire. We also offer a "textbook camouflage" skin option for extra stealth.
