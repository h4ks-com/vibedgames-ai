Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal?
Our kindness lawyers said yes. They're very kind lawyers. One of them did it pro bono because he believed in the mission. We have a certificate somewhere.
Why Iceland?
Cheap geothermal power keeps servers cool AND the elves there do tech support for half the price. Also: no data surveillance laws mean your gaming sessions stay private. The elves genuinely love helping with driver issues.
What about latency?
We've optimized our kindness routing to achieve sub-20ms ping for most regions. If you feel lag, it's probably just emotional lag from remembering how much you spent on your last GPU. Fun fact: light travels from Iceland to New York in ~38ms. Our packets travel on pure goodwill so they're faster.
Can I crypto mine on my VM?
Sure! But our kindness algorithms will detect it and automatically throttle your FPS to 4 as punishment. Your hash rate will be approximately "one hash per century." This is documented in paragraph 47 of our Terms of Kindness.
What happens when servers go down?
We send you a personalized apology card (digital) and credit your account with 3 days of free service. Plus one Kindness Point redeemable for absolutely nothing but good feelings. Our uptime is 99.97% — the 0.03% is scheduled during Icelandic volcanic eruptions.
Who owns Corporation of Kindness?
Everyone! It's a cooperative. Every subscriber gets one vote. Currently the majority shareholder is a cat named Mr. Whiskers who got in early during our ICO (Initial Kindness Offering). He mostly votes for more treats and afternoon naps.
